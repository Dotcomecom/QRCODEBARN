QR welcome pack for Airbnb hosts and holiday cottage owners

Use this free tool to create a printable QR code welcome pack for a holiday cottage, Airbnb, B&B, guest house, serviced apartment, glamping pod or farm stay. Add useful guest links such as WiFi, house manual, emergency contacts, local pubs, dog walks, check-out instructions and things to do nearby.

For Airbnb hosts

Replace repeated guest questions with one branded QR sheet beside the kettle, on the coffee table or inside your welcome folder.

For holiday cottages

Create a guest-friendly printed pack with WiFi access, house rules, local recommendations, taxi numbers and emergency details.

For B&Bs and guest houses

Share breakfast times, local attractions, review links, parking instructions and direct booking links without printing long manuals.
