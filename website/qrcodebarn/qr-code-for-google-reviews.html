What this QR code is for

Help customers reach your review page quickly with a printed QR code. A good QR code page should open quickly, be mobile-friendly and clearly match the promise printed beside the code.

Best places to use it

  • Printed posters and table signs
  • Business cards and flyers
  • Reception desks, counters and packaging
  • Email signatures and presentation slides

How to make it scan reliably

Use strong contrast, leave a quiet margin around the code and avoid placing it on curved or reflective surfaces. For printed material, test it with more than one phone before producing a large batch.
