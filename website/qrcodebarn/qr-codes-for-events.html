QR Codes for Events

Event QR codes can support tickets, schedules, maps, registration forms, feedback surveys, WiFi access and follow-up marketing.

Before the event

Use QR codes on posters, flyers and social posts to send people to the registration page, event details or calendar entry.

During the event

At the venue, QR codes can help guests find schedules, speaker bios, maps, menus, WiFi access and live feedback forms.

After the event

Use QR codes on handouts or signs to collect feedback, share slides, promote future events or send attendees to a mailing list signup page.

Operational tips

Place codes at eye level where possible. Avoid reflective surfaces, poor lighting and tiny print sizes. Add clear text explaining what each scan will do.
