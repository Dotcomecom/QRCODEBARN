QR Code Generator for Business

Businesses use QR codes to connect offline materials to online actions.

Quick answer

  • Use QR codes where they save customers time.
  • Add clear labels next to every code.
  • Track important campaigns where possible.

Business use cases

QR codes are useful on shop windows, flyers, invoices, receipts, packaging, business cards and event stands.

Calls to action

Add a clear instruction such as Scan to book, Scan for menu or Scan for reviews.

Customer trust

Tell users what will happen after scanning.

Testing before launch

Test the code under real conditions: printed size, lighting, distance and likely phone models.
