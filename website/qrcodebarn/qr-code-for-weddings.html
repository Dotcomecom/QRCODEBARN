QR Codes for Weddings

Practical QR code ideas for weddings, including where to place codes, what to link to and how to avoid scanning problems.

Useful QR code ideas for Weddings

  • Link to booking, enquiry or contact pages.
  • Share menus, brochures, price lists, forms or instructions.
  • Collect reviews, feedback or photo uploads.
  • Show maps, opening hours, social profiles or event information.

Recommended QR types

Most weddings use static QR codes for stable links such as websites, documents and profile pages. Use tracked or dynamic links when you need analytics, campaign labels or the ability to change the destination later.

Placement checklist

Put the code where the customer already has a reason to scan: a table, reception desk, window, counter, invoice, flyer, vehicle, sign or email confirmation. Add a short call to action such as “Scan for menu” or “Scan to book”.
