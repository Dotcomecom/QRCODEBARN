QR Codes for Beauty Salons

Practical QR code ideas for beauty salons, including where to place codes, what to link to and how to avoid scanning problems.

Ad space

A practical beauty salons QR workflow

Beauty salons often need QR codes that make booking, service menus and review requests feel effortless. A well-placed code can remove friction at the moment someone is already deciding whether to book.

Useful QR links for Beauty Salons

  • Online booking page for appointments and add-ons
  • Service menu with prices, timings and treatment notes
  • Instagram gallery or before-and-after portfolio
  • Google review request after appointment confirmation
  • Gift voucher or membership sign-up page
  • Stylist profile page or consultation booking form

Where to place the code

  • Reception desk: booking link and service menu
  • Mirror or treatment room door: stylist profiles and offers
  • Salon window: Instagram page and new-client booking
  • Business cards: appointment booking and contact details
  • Email confirmation: review request and loyalty sign-up

Recommended QR types

Use a static QR code for a permanent service menu, booking page or social link. If you want to change the destination after printing or track which offer gets the best response, choose a dynamic or tracked QR code.

Common mistakes and practical checks

  • Avoid small codes on glossy mirrors where reflections reduce scanning
  • Do not use the same code for every location without a clear action label
  • Test the code after cleaning or installing the sign
  • Make sure the mobile page opens quickly and shows prices clearly
