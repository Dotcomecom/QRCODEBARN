QR Codes for Car Dealers

Practical QR code ideas for car dealers, including where to place codes, what to link to and how to avoid scanning problems.

Ad space

A practical car dealers QR workflow

Car dealers can use QR codes to connect showroom visitors to stock details, test-drive booking and review requests. Each code should point to a real next step that matches the place it is displayed.

Useful QR links for Car Dealers

  • Vehicle page with photos, finance options and mileage details
  • Test-drive booking form from the forecourt or showroom
  • Trade-in enquiry form for quick follow-up
  • Google review request after a sale or service visit
  • Video walkthrough or 360-degree view for online stock
  • Opening-hours and location page for walk-in visitors

Where to place the code

  • Vehicle windscreen or display card: stock details and finance link
  • Forecourt sign: test-drive booking and contact page
  • Reception desk: review request and trade-in form
  • Service bay: booking page and workshop contact
  • Brochures: vehicle walkaround and call-to-action

Recommended QR types

A static QR code works well for a fixed vehicle page or showroom contact page. Use a dynamic or tracked QR code for campaigns tied to a specific promotion, model launch or staff member.

Common mistakes and practical checks

  • Do not link a vehicle code to a generic homepage when the buyer needs a specific page
  • Avoid codes that are too small on window stickers or forecourt boards
  • Test the link on a phone outdoors in bright light before printing
  • Label the code with a specific action such as “Scan for test drive”
