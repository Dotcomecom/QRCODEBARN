QR Codes for Restaurants

Restaurants can use QR codes for menus, bookings, reviews, WiFi access, allergen information, loyalty schemes and takeaway ordering.

Digital menus

Menu QR codes reduce the need to print new menus whenever prices, dishes or availability change. They are especially useful for seasonal menus, wine lists and specials.

Booking and review links

A QR code on a receipt, table card or exit sign can send guests to a booking page, TripAdvisor profile, Google review page or feedback form.

Allergen and dietary information

A QR code can link to a live allergen page. This is useful because allergen information needs to be clear, accurate and easy to update.

Placement tips

Place restaurant QR codes on table tents, menus, windows, receipts and takeaway packaging. Use a clear call to action such as “Scan for today’s menu” or “Scan to leave a review”.
