QR Code Size Guide

QR code size depends on scanning distance. Small close-range uses, such as business cards and table menus, can work with compact codes. Posters, windows and event signage need larger codes because users scan from further away.

As a rule, leave a clear quiet zone around the QR code. Avoid crowding it with borders, photos, icons or text. The quiet zone helps phones separate the code from the surrounding design.

Before printing in bulk, test a sample at the real printed size, on the actual material, under realistic lighting. Glossy surfaces, folds and low contrast can all reduce scan reliability.
