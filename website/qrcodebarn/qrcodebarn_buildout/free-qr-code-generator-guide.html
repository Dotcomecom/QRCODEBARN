Free QR Code Generator Guide

A free QR code generator converts a short piece of information into a scannable square code. The information can be a web address, contact card, WiFi login, text block, email address, phone number, map link or menu page.

The most reliable QR codes are simple. A short URL generally scans faster than a very long URL. If you are printing a QR code on a leaflet, poster, menu or business card, generate the code, scan it with more than one phone, and only then send the artwork to print.

For businesses, the best approach is usually to point the QR code at a landing page rather than putting too much data inside the code itself. That way the QR code stays clean and the page can be updated later.
