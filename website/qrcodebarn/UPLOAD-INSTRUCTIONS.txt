QRCodeBarn function fix pack

This pack fixes two pages:

1) qr-code-for-business-card.html
- Creates a working vCard/business card QR code.
- Removes the bug where placeholder text stayed over the generated QR code.
- Adds a PNG download button.

2) qr-code-with-logo.html
- Adds a logo upload field.
- Generates a QR code with the uploaded logo placed in the centre.
- Adds size/colour controls and PNG download.

Upload instructions:
- Extract this ZIP on your computer.
- Upload both HTML files directly into the qrcodebarn.com public_html folder.
- Overwrite the existing files when asked.
- Do not upload the enclosing folder itself.

After upload test:
https://www.qrcodebarn.com/qr-code-for-business-card.html
https://www.qrcodebarn.com/qr-code-with-logo.html
