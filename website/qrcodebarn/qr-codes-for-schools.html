QR Codes for Schools

Schools can use QR codes to share forms, newsletters, event details, homework links, maps and parent information pages.

Parent communications

QR codes can help parents reach forms, letters and payment pages without typing long URLs from printed handouts.

Classroom use

Teachers can use QR codes to link to learning resources, quizzes, reading materials, videos and project instructions.

Events and open days

Schools can use QR codes on posters and welcome packs to share maps, schedules, safeguarding information and feedback forms.

Safeguarding and privacy

Avoid linking public QR codes to sensitive information. Use secure systems where personal data or pupil information is involved.
