QR Codes for Product Packaging

QR codes on packaging can connect customers to instructions, authenticity checks, recipes, warranty registration, recycling information and support.

Useful packaging destinations

Product packaging QR codes often link to setup guides, demonstration videos, care instructions, safety information, recipes, manuals or replacement parts.

Keep URLs durable

Packaging can remain in circulation for months or years. Use stable URLs and keep control of the domain so the code does not become obsolete.

Design and scanning

Avoid placing QR codes across folds, seams or highly curved areas. Test the code on the final packaging material.

Customer support

A QR code that links to troubleshooting or warranty registration can reduce support friction and improve the customer experience.
