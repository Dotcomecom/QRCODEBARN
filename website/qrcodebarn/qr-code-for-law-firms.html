QR Codes for Law Firms

Practical QR code ideas for law firms, including where to place codes, what to link to and how to avoid scanning problems.

Ad space

A practical law firms QR workflow

Law firms can use QR codes to make consultations, contact details and document requests easier to access. The code should point to a page that feels calm, clear and mobile-friendly.

Useful QR links for Law Firms

  • Consultation booking or enquiry form
  • Contact page with office address and opening hours
  • Client guide or downloadable brochure
  • Review request after a successful matter
  • Service area or expertise page
  • Email signature or business card link

Where to place the code

  • Reception desk: contact and booking link
  • Business cards: consultation booking and profile page
  • Brochures: expertise page and service overview
  • Office window: office address and opening hours
  • Email signatures: review or contact link

Recommended QR types

Use a static QR code for a permanent contact page or service overview. A dynamic or tracked QR code suits campaigns or promotions that may change over time.

Common mistakes and practical checks

  • Avoid sending clients to a generic homepage when they need a consultation page
  • Do not place the code where it is too small for a brochure or business card
  • Test the link from a phone before printing the final design
  • Keep the instruction short and professional
