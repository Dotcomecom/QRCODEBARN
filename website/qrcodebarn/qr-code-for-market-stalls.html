QR Codes for Market Stalls

Practical QR code ideas for market stalls, including where to place codes, what to link to and how to avoid scanning problems.

Ad space

A practical market stalls QR workflow

Market stalls can use QR codes to connect shoppers to price lists, social pages and ordering details quickly. A code should be visible and useful without slowing down a busy transaction.

Useful QR links for Market Stalls

  • Price list or product catalogue page
  • Instagram or social media follow link
  • Collection or preorder instructions
  • Google review request after the purchase
  • Contact page for wholesale enquiries
  • Event appearance or market location updates

Where to place the code

  • Front of stall: prices and product menu
  • Packaging or receipt: social follow and review link
  • Signboard: collection instructions and highlights
  • Card or flyer: preorder or wholesale enquiry
  • Event handout: upcoming dates and location info

Recommended QR types

Use a static QR code for a fixed price list or social page. A dynamic or tracked code works better if you rotate offers, change market locations or want to track which stall messages get the most scans.

Common mistakes and practical checks

  • Avoid placing the code where it is hidden behind display items
  • Do not link to a long PDF without a clear action label
  • Test the code in bright daylight and in busier conditions
  • Keep the text around the code short and easy to read
