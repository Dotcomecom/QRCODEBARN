About QRCodeBarn

QRCodeBarn provides free QR code tools, templates and practical QR guides for everyday business use.

QRCodeBarn is a free utility site focused on simple QR code creation and practical education. The site is designed for small businesses, restaurants, event organisers, schools, property owners and creators who need quick QR codes without a complicated workflow.

We focus on static QR code generation, print reliability, template ideas and clear guidance for safe scanning.
