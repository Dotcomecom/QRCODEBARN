QR Codes for Real Estate Agents

Estate agents can use QR codes on boards, brochures and window cards to send buyers to property details, virtual tours and booking forms.

Property brochures

A QR code can connect a printed brochure to a live listing page with more photos, floorplans, EPC details and viewing availability.

For sale boards

QR codes on boards can be useful when the property has strong passing footfall. The destination should be mobile-friendly and load quickly.

Viewing bookings

A code can send prospects to a calendar or enquiry form, reducing friction and making it easier to capture interest outside office hours.

Best practice

Use one clear destination per code and keep the listing page updated. Remove or redirect old listing URLs after a property is sold or let.
