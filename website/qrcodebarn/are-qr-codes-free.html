Are QR Codes Free?

Many QR codes are free to create, but the answer depends on whether the code is static or dynamic.

Quick answer

  • Static QR codes can usually be created for free.
  • Dynamic QR codes often use paid services.
  • Check the terms before printing important codes.

Static QR codes

A static QR code contains the destination directly and usually remains usable without a paid service.

Dynamic QR codes

A dynamic QR code usually relies on a redirect service that may charge for editing or analytics.

Why free tools exist

Simple QR codes are easy to generate with standard encoding methods.

Before you print

Make sure you know whether your code depends on a paid account.
