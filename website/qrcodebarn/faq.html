QR Code FAQ

Frequently asked questions about creating, printing, testing and using QR codes.

Are QR codes free?

Static QR codes can be free. Dynamic QR code services often charge because they host redirects, analytics or editing features.

Do QR codes expire?

A static QR code does not expire by itself, but the destination link can break if the page is moved or deleted.

Can I print a QR code?

Yes. Use strong contrast, adequate size and test before printing in volume.

Can I change a QR code later?

Only if the QR points to a destination you control or uses a dynamic redirect service.
