QR Codes for Vets

Useful QR code ideas for veterinary clinics, including emergency contact details, appointment booking, clinic location and pet-care guidance.

Ad space

A practical veterinary QR workflow

Veterinary clinics can use QR codes to make common tasks faster for pet owners while reducing the need for printed paperwork. A single code can connect people to the right information in seconds.

Useful QR links for a vet clinic

  • Emergency contact details and out-of-hours guidance
  • Appointment booking or online enquiry form
  • Clinic location, parking and opening hours
  • Pet-care instructions and aftercare documents
  • Review requests and feedback forms

Create a veterinary QR code

Common veterinary QR examples

  • Reception desk: emergency contact details and clinic location
  • Waiting room: appointment booking and new client forms
  • Discharge notes: aftercare instructions and medication reminders
  • Practice posters: review requests and social media links
  • Pet-care packs: feeding guidance, vaccination reminders and follow-up links

Best practices

Keep the destination clear, test every QR code before printing and make sure the linked page is mobile-friendly, easy to read and relevant to the place where the code is displayed.
