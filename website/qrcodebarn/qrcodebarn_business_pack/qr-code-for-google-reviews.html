How review QR codes work

Find the public link for your Google Business Profile review page, then paste it into the generator. The QR code sends customers straight to the review flow or business profile depending on your link.

Where to use review QR codes

Review QR codes work well when customers have just had a positive experience. Add them to receipts, table cards, appointment cards, takeaway packaging, invoices or post-service thank-you notes.

Review request best practice

Ask honestly and avoid pressure. Do not offer rewards for positive reviews. The safest wording is neutral, for example: “Enjoyed your visit? Scan to leave us a review.”

Frequently asked questions

Can I create a QR code for Google reviews?
Yes. Use your Google review link as the QR code destination.
Where should I display a review QR code?
Receipts, table cards, appointment cards, invoices and packaging are common placements.
Can I ask only happy customers for reviews?
It is better to ask customers neutrally and avoid pressuring people to leave only positive reviews.
Do review QR codes help local businesses?
They can reduce friction and make it easier for customers to find the right review page.
