QR Code for Google Drive

Create and use a QR code that sends people to your Google Drive link, profile, form, payment page or shared document.

How to use a Google Drive QR code

Copy the public link you want people to open, paste it into the QR generator and test the result on a mobile phone. Make sure the destination page is not private or blocked by login requirements unless that is intentional.

Best uses

  • Posters, signs and printed flyers
  • Business cards and packaging
  • Events, shops and reception areas
  • Email signatures and printed documents

Tracking tip

For marketing campaigns, use a short tracked URL or UTM-tagged link before creating the QR code. That makes scan traffic easier to identify in analytics.
