About QRCodeBarn

QRCodeBarn was created to provide a simple free QR code generator and practical QR code guides for people who need clear, reliable QR codes without unnecessary complexity.

The site focuses on everyday QR code use cases including business cards, restaurants, events, schools, product packaging, marketing campaigns and general website links.

Our goal

Our goal is to help users create QR codes that are easy to scan, clearly labelled and linked to useful destinations.

What we publish

QRCodeBarn publishes QR code guides, FAQs, best-practice articles and simple tools. We aim to keep the content practical, readable and useful for non-technical users.
