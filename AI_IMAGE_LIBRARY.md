# AI_IMAGE_LIBRARY.md

## Purpose

Production-quality image prompts for every QRCodeBarn industry hero image.

Use these prompts to generate consistent, premium hero visuals that fit the QRCodeBarn brand:

- modern SaaS / editorial hybrid
- clean, high-trust composition
- bright, premium lighting
- useful negative space for headline overlays
- no visible text, no watermarks, no device mockups that dominate the frame
- aspect ratio: 16:9 or 3:2
- style: photographic, polished, realistic, minimal clutter

Use this base style in every prompt:

## Usage Notes

1. Keep the final output free of text overlays in the generated image.
2. Reserve negative space for the website hero heading and intro copy.
3. Match the page topic through objects, color palette, and environment rather than by adding words inside the image.
4. Prefer a realistic, editorial, premium look over abstract AI-art styling.
5. Avoid visible logos or branded UI that would create licensing or trademark issues.

## Recommended Output Format

- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Resolution: 2048 px wide minimum
- Style: photographic or highly realistic editorial render
- Lighting: bright natural or soft studio light
- Composition: subject left or right with open space for headline overlay
